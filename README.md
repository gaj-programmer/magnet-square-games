# Privacy Policy for Ant Tapper

**Last Updated: December 28, 2024**

**Developed by: MagnetSquare Games**

## Introduction

MagnetSquare Games ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game "Ant Tapper" (the "Game").

By downloading, installing, or playing Ant Tapper, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Game.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Ant Tapper is designed with your privacy in mind. We do **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media files
- Financial or payment information
- Social media account information
- Device identifiers for advertising purposes

### Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers:

- **High Scores**: Your game scores for each difficulty level are saved locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system.
- **Game Settings**: Any game preferences you set are stored locally.

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

All game data (high scores and settings) is stored locally on your device using standard mobile application storage methods. This data:

- Is not uploaded to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Is not used for analytics or advertising
- Will be deleted if you uninstall the Game

## Third-Party Services

### Advertising

**Ant Tapper does NOT contain any advertisements.** We do not use any advertising SDKs or collect any data for advertising purposes.

### Analytics

**Ant Tapper does NOT use any analytics services.** We do not track your gameplay, behavior, or usage patterns.

## Children's Privacy

Ant Tapper does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The Game is suitable for all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Parents can be assured that:
- No personal data is collected from children
- No data is shared with third parties
- The Game contains no advertising

## Permissions

Ant Tapper may request the following device permissions:

- **Storage (if applicable)**: Only used to save game data locally on your device. This data is never transmitted externally.

## Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data retention policy applicable to user data on our servers. Local game data (high scores) remains on your device until you:

- Clear the app data
- Uninstall the Game
- Reset your device

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. You can delete all locally stored game data by:

1. Going to your device Settings
2. Finding Ant Tapper in your Apps list
3. Selecting "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage"

Alternatively, uninstalling the Game will remove all associated data.
